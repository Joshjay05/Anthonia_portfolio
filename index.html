About Dr. Omolola

Professional Experience

Summary of Professional Background

With extensive experience as an international languages teacher and education expert, Dr. Eddo has worked in diverse cultural contexts across Nigeria, the Middle East, and West Africa, specifically in Nigeria, Ghana, Benin Republic, and the United Kingdom. Her career in education spans several years, during which she has developed a strong foundation in teaching various languages, including English, French, and Mandarin, fostering linguistic and cultural exchange between different regions.

Teaching and Education Experience

Dr. Eddo holds formal training and certifications in education from reputable institutions in Nigeria, Ghana, Benin Republic, and the United Kingdom. Her academic qualifications have provided her with a deep understanding of diverse teaching methodologies and educational systems. These qualifications have equipped her with the skills necessary to adapt her teaching style to cater to the unique needs of students from different cultural backgrounds and educational settings.

Business Project in Agriculture and Healthy Food Production

Alongside her teaching career, Dr. Eddo is actively involved in a business venture focused on sustainable agriculture, healthy food production, and marketing. This project aims to address food security and promote the consumption of nutritious, locally produced food. The initiative involves working with local farmers to promote organic farming techniques, the use of environmentally friendly agricultural practices, and creating a network for distributing fresh, healthy produce to local markets.

Secretary General of the Nigerians in the Middle East Community (NGO)

Dr. Eddo serves as the Secretary General of the Nigerians in the Middle East Community, a non-governmental organization dedicated to supporting Nigerian nationals living and working in the Middle East. As Secretary General, she is responsible for overseeing the administrative functions of the organization, coordinating events, managing communication, and engaging with various stakeholders to ensure the success of the community's initiatives.

Her career spans teaching, business, and community leadership, with a focus on education, sustainable agriculture, and social impact. She brings a unique blend of international experience, entrepreneurial spirit, and a strong commitment to improving the lives of others through education and community service.
